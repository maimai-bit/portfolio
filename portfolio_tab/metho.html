Case Study: Metho – Minimalist Planning for a Methodic Life

Overview

In a world saturated with productivity apps that overwhelm users with cluttered dashboards and endless lists, Metho offers a refreshing alternative. Developed with a methodic mindset, Metho is a minimalist to-do and agenda app designed for people who want clarity, not chaos.

The Problem

Traditional productivity apps often suffer from over-engineering. For many, especially neurodivergent users or those who simply crave focus, this results in more stress than structure. As a busy teacher, I experienced this firsthand. I often felt overwhelmed by how other apps displayed everything simultaneously. I needed something quieter—something that helped me stay on track without shouting for my attention.

My Role

I designed and built Metho from the ground up. The name comes from the word methodic, reflecting a structured yet gentle approach to productivity.

Feature Walkthrough

Events – Plan without pressure

Goal: Allow users to plan without clutter or stress.

  • Minimal, intuitive input layout
  • No cluttered timeline or noisy reminders
  • Clear separation of past, current, and upcoming events

Habits – Build consistency with ease

Goal: Help users stick with habits naturally.

  • Gentle progress visuals without pressure
  • Only today’s habits shown—no future overload
  • Simple tracking, easy to edit

Routines – Structure your day gently

Goal: Enable flow with calm daily structure.

  • Quick-start templates for common routines
  • Customize routines to your lifestyle
  • No pressure to complete—just flow

Why It Works

Unlike most apps, Metho doesn’t overload you with lists or deadlines. You only see what matters now. That’s it. This reduction of visual clutter leads to more focused, less anxious days.
